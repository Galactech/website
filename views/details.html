What is this all about?

Robotics

Robotics is the combination of important skills, like hardware, coding, and outreach. The combination of these skills will be refined to outperform other teams. You will strategize ways to meet requirments.

FTC

FIRST is a Nonprofit aimed at delivering robotics oppurtunities. FTC is one of it's three branches. FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge and builds medium sized robots with of high school students.

This Team

Many high schools offer the oppurtunity to join a FTC team representing the school. This team however is a private team, based on Newcastle Washington, and recruit members on an interview basis.

What is robotics about?

Robotics encomapsses many skills that the future generation will have to use. Outreach, Coding, and Building are the three main tools.

In FTC, Outreach will focus on luring investors, presenting the robot, and inspiring younger kids. Coding will focus on providing functionality to the built robot. Building will construct a robot from parts bought. Robotics is a very general term though, and these tasks and responsibilities vary.

What is FTC about?

A division of robotics created with the nonprofit FIRST, along with FLL and FRC.

FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge and builds medium sized robots with a medium sized group of high school students. Every fall a new challenge gets released for all FTC teams. These teams kick off their school year by racing towards building the best possible robot. Using existing and future funds, they build a competitive robot for the next many months, with many competitions and roadblocks in between.

What is Galactech all about?

Galactech Robotics is a private FTC team assembled in 2022 with 8 members: Ethan Lee, Sidd Woddi, Johaan Mannanal, Lena Manjali, Ishaan Mannanal, Jaden Yu, Eli Kim, and Pranav Sankar

Galactech focuses on year-round quality work, and strives to offer the best robots and community possible. We are based in Renton, Washington. If you do end up wanting to join a FTC team, and you do not have a school team accessible look for a private team. Galactech also is open for new members but recruits on an interview basis. If joining an existing private is though, a great idea is to make one with friends.
